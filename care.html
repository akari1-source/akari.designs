Care Instructions

Preserving the beauty of your handcrafted keepsake

Do's

  • Display away from direct sunlight
  • Dust gently with a soft, dry cloth
  • Keep in a stable, room-temperature environment
  • Handle with clean, dry hands
  • Place on stable surfaces away from edges

Don'ts

  • Expose to direct sunlight for extended periods
  • Use harsh chemicals, abrasives, or solvents
  • Submerge in water or expose to moisture
  • Place near extreme heat sources
  • Drop or handle roughly

Long-Term Care

With proper care, your AKARI piece will remain beautiful for years

🌤️

Light Exposure

While our materials are UV-resistant, prolonged direct sunlight may cause slight fading over many years. Display in indirect light when possible.

🧹

Cleaning

Dust regularly with a soft microfiber cloth. For deeper cleaning, use a slightly damp cloth followed by immediate drying. Avoid sprays.

🌡️

Environment

Keep away from radiators, fireplaces, and extreme temperature changes. Ideal room temperature is 18–24°C with moderate humidity.
