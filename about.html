Meet the Maker

Hi, I'm Heena. As a mother, I've come to understand just how precious life's little moments are. A wedding bouquet, a baby's first flowers, a meaningful gift from someone special—these memories deserve to be cherished long after the moment has passed.

That's what inspired me to create AKARI Designs. Every piece is individually handcrafted by me in my Berlin studio—never mass-produced, never outsourced. Whether I'm preserving flowers, creating personalized letters, or designing custom keepsakes, every creation is made as if it were for my own family.

My goal is simple: to transform meaningful memories into timeless keepsakes that can be treasured for years to come.

Behind the Scenes

In our light-filled Berlin workshop, the process is entirely hands-on. Flowers arrive fresh and full of meaning, then are carefully dried, arranged, and set into crystal-clear material—each step performed slowly and deliberately, by hand.

Every piece passes through Heena's hands from start to finish—from the first sketch to the final polish. No assembly lines, no shortcuts—just one maker, devoted to your memory.

Our Berlin Studio

A creative space where memories are carefully kept

Tucked away in Berlin, our studio is a calm, sunlit workspace filled with dried botanicals, sketches, and the quiet focus of slow craft. It's where your flowers are welcomed, honored, and transformed into something that lasts.

We take great care in every step—from how your flowers are received to how your finished keepsake is wrapped and sent. Craftsmanship here is personal, patient, and made to be treasured.

AKARI Designs is a legally registered business in Germany, based in Berlin.
