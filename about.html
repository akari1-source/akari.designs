About AKARI

The story behind our handcrafted keepsakes

Our Mission

At AKARI Designs, we believe every flower tells a story. Every bouquet marks a milestone. Every petal holds a memory worth keeping.

Based in Berlin, our studio transforms fleeting botanical moments into timeless handcrafted keepsakes. From wedding bouquets and baby's first flowers to memorial tributes and corporate celebrations, we preserve what matters most in premium crystal-clear material, created entirely by hand.

Our Story

What started as a passion for flower preservation has grown into a Berlin-based studio dedicated to creating meaningful, handcrafted keepsakes that tell your unique story.

Meet the Maker

Crafting timeless keepsakes, one memory at a time.

Hi, I'm Heena, the maker behind Akari Designs. As a mother, I've come to understand just how precious life's little moments are. A wedding bouquet, a baby's first flowers, a meaningful gift from someone special—these memories deserve to be cherished long after the moment has passed.

That's what inspired me to create Akari Designs. Every piece is handcrafted by me with patience, love, and attention to detail. Whether I'm preserving flowers, creating personalized letters, or designing custom keepsakes, every creation is made as if it were for my own family.

My goal is simple: to transform meaningful memories into timeless keepsakes that can be treasured for years to come. Thank you for trusting me to preserve your special moments.

Behind the Scenes

In our light-filled Berlin workshop, the process is entirely hands-on. Flowers arrive fresh and full of meaning, then are carefully dried, arranged, and set into crystal-clear material—each step performed slowly and deliberately, by hand.

Every piece passes through Heena's hands from start to finish—from the first sketch to the final polish. No assembly lines, no shortcuts—just one maker, devoted to your memory.

Our Berlin Studio

A creative space where memories are carefully kept

Tucked away in Berlin, our studio is a calm, sunlit workspace filled with dried botanicals, sketches, and the quiet focus of slow craft. It's where your flowers are welcomed, honored, and transformed into something that lasts.

We take great care in every step—from how your flowers are received to how your finished keepsake is wrapped and sent. Craftsmanship here is personal, patient, and made to be treasured.
